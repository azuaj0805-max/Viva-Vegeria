Our Story

We are a fully from-scratch kitchen making delicious vegan and gluten-free Tex-Mex inspired meals. We pride ourselves in offering our community healthier options while still maintaining an authenticity and flavor that is true to Mexican culture. We source our ingredients fresh every day and make our recipes in house, because we shouldn't have to compromise the flavors that we love to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

When Viva opened its doors well a decade ago we were san antonios first fully vegan tex mex restaurant. We are proud of our heritage and take pride it the food we put on the table for the people who decide to go through our doors. We do what we do for the love we have for san antonio and our local community. Viva is family owned and operated and we would welcome anyone who decides to enter the doors of 1422 Nogalitos st to be part of our family as well.
